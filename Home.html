Research Interests:

    Full-Duplex communication
The co-time co-frequency full-duplex communication has the potentials to double the spectral eficiency of the current systems. I am mainly focused on performance analysis and optimization, hybrid half-/full-duplex protocol design, resource llocation and multi-user scheduling of full-duplex systems.

    Radar-Communication convergence
The frequency sharing between radar and communication is a envisioned in the future for the many new applications, such as unmanned driving, requires both operations. For this topic, I mainly focused on the systems design, protocols design, performance bounds analysis, etc.
